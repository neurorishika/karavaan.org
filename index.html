GREEN SCREEN

A game that comprises of a lot of fun, for both the participants as well as the audience.

RULES

  1. The participant is blindfolded and must attempt guessing a video playing in the background.
  2. The video will be played (and replayed) for a time-window of two minutes.
  3. Within this time, the judges will suggest what the video is about by making witty puns.
  4. The participant should converse with the judges and try to guess the video or what it is about within the two minutes.
  5. During the conversation, coherence should be maintained, and statements shouldn't be inserted randomly.
  6. The videos cover the genres of science fiction, current affairs in science and pop-culture.

Registration Fees - None

Tarantallegra

Karavaan brings to you "Tarantallegra", an Inter-Collegiate Online Dance Competition spanning the Nation. So delay no more, let the cornucopia of music flow and twirl away to your desired dance style. Dont forget to upload a video and stand a chance of winning a cash prize of 10 K along with 5K for runners up.

RULES

  • The competition will be held online. Participants are to submit their entries by uploading them to YouTube with the hashtags "#Karavaan" & "#Tarantallegra18" and also submitting the link to the event portal. There is no restriction on the form of dance in the submission.
  • Valid Student IDs of accredited colleges must be submitted along with the participation video as identity proof.
  • The competition is for group performances only, with a group consisting of 5-10 members. An individual may not be a member of more than one group. A group may submit only one video.
  • Multiple groups from one college, as well as groups made across colleges, are allowed.
  • The duration of the performance must be between 3 and 7 minutes.
  • The video should be in a single frame, in a single shot, with resolution no less than 720p. All members and steps in the video should be clearly visible.
  • The videos submitted maybe used for promotional purposes.
  • Criteria for Judgement will be Choreography, Coordination, Creativity, Power of Portrayal and WOW factor.
  • There are no restriction on usage of props
  • Violation of any of the rules will lead to disqualification.

Registration Fee: Rs. 100/- per team.
Last Date for Registration: October 24

Pronite

Live Performances

About Pronite:

Pronite is definitely one of the (if not the) most awaited events of Karavaan. Traditionally the last night of Karavaan, Pronite is when everyone, students and faculty alike, come out to the Main Stage on the cricket ground and cut loose for a night of great music, delicious food and unending fun. With Pronites in previous years boasting of the likes of Indian Ocean, The Local Train, Lagori and Sorabh Pant, it's not difficult to see why Pronite is an unforgettable part of every Karavaan. Professional musicians and performers take to the stage to light up the evening, providing a perfect finale to Karavaan.
This year, Pronite will be on 4th November, and we have an exciting line-up of performances this year too. So save the date!

About the Artists:

Agam

Pioneers in the genre of Carnatic fusion rock, Agam has taken the world by storm, enthralling fans everywhere with their unique brand of music. Marrying soulful traditional vocals with the punch of guitars and drums, Agam creates magic from an unlikely fusion. They manage to weave progressive rock and classical music into a rich and vibrant tapestry of melody and rhythm, and simultaneously connect you to your roots, as well as transport you to another world.
This year, Agam will be taking to the stage on Pronite at Karavaan'18! It promises to be an unforgettable performance, so make sure you don't miss it. Head over to the cricket stadium in IISER Pune in the evening on the 4th of November, and prepare to be dazzled.

About

Karavaan is the annual science, cultural, and social fest of IISER Pune. Born in 2008 as a small intra-college festival, it has grown with each edition and has now matured into something far greater.

The 11th edition of Karavaan is scheduled to occur on 2nd, 3rd and 4th of November, 2018. These three days will be sprawling with a plethora of competitions and activities spanning areas like music, dance, photography, science, quizzing, drama, literary, and general tomfoolery. Eminent personalities from all over India from niche fields will also be invited to join us in a number of much-awaited talk series and workshops on a variety of subjects on the days leading up to, as well as during Karavaan. The theme for this year, "Fandom and Fiction" , will in no small measure, leave a mark on the minds of everyone who joins us for the fest that is Karavaan.
